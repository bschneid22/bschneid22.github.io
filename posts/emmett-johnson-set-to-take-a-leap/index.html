Emmett Johnson has had an impressive redshirt freshman and sophomore seasons in Nebraska’s backfield. The junior running back is on pace to become an elite tailback.

Here is a comparison to three former Husker running backs for their first two seasons that had success in Lincoln:

Starting with rushing statistics, Johnson is on track to become the elite running back that former Nebraska running backs Ameer Abdullah, Rex Burkhead and Roy Helu were.
